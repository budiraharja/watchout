How To:
  • Go To Settings:

    Menus will be shown by dragging/moving camera preview frame anywhere. Drag it to the icon until the icon turns and then drop it.

  • Zoom (Supported Device Only):

    • Default zoom scale can be updated in Settings.

    • Check on "Display Zoom Shortcut" to add zoom shortcut to main preview.

    • Zoom In

      Menus will be shown by dragging/moving camera preview frame anywhere. Drag it to the icon until the icon turns to and then drop it.

    • Zoom Out

      Menus will be shown by dragging/moving camera preview frame anywhere. Drag it to the icon until the icon turns to and then drop it.

  • Close The Application:

    Menus will be shown by dragging/moving camera preview frame anywhere. Drag it to the icon until the icon turns to and then drop it.
